About this website

"Just come out to dinner tonight!" my friend implored me.

"No, if I save the $30 today that can make me a lot more money later on."

While I do not recommend anyone act like me while speaking to friends, that conversation inspired this website.

How much money would that $30 be in 10 years, or 30 years? This page was designed to help answer such questions. Even though investment performance is not perfectly predictable, this website gives helpful estimates for how much investments increase in value over time.

This website contains a series of investment calculators designed to show you the long term impact of various investment decisions. The calculators build on one another and are intended to be reviewed from top to bottom, but you can view them in any order you choose.
