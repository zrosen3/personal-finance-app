The capital gains tax is a tax on the amount by which your investment increased. This tax is incurred when you sell an investment. Capital gains tax is higher for long-term versus short-term investments, where long-term is defined by an investment held for more than one year.

The short term capital gains tax rate is the same as the federal income tax rate, which is usually higher than the long term capital gains rate. For the calculator above, we are calculating the long term capital gains tax rate for investments sold in 2022. This calculator was based on Nerd Wallet's helpful table which can be found here.
